MY CAREER ROADMAP PLAN

1. Entry-Level Position: Network Technician

Job Requirements and Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in information technology or related field.
  • Basic understanding of network infrastructure and hardware.
  • Proficiency in troubleshooting network issues.
  • Knowledge of LANs, WANs, and network protocols.
  • Entry-level network certifications (e.g., CompTIA Network+).
  • Strong problem-solving skills.
 

2. Next Step: Junior Network Engineer

Job Requirements and Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in information technology with a focus on network engineering.
  • Experience as a network technician or in a similar role.
  • Proficiency in configuring and managing network devices.
  • Familiarity with LAN, WAN, WLAN, and network security.
  • Network certifications (e.g., CCNA).
  • Strong analytical and troubleshooting skills.
 

3. Career Progression: Network Engineer

Job Requirements and Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in information technology or related field.
  • Several years of experience in network roles, including junior network engineer.
  • In-depth knowledge of network infrastructure and protocols.
  • Proficiency in network design and implementation.
  • Advanced network certifications (e.g., CCNP).
  • Strong project management skills.
 

4. Target Career: Cybersecurity Engineer

Job Requirements and Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in computer science or a related field.
  • Prior experience in network engineering or related roles.
  • Strong understanding of cybersecurity principles.
  • Proficiency in security tools and technologies.
  • Relevant cybersecurity certifications (e.g., CompTIA Security+, CISSP).
  • Incident detection and response experience.
  • Scripting and programming skills (e.g., Python).
 

5. Career Advancement: Senior Cybersecurity Engineer

Job Requirements and Qualifications

  • Extensive experience in cybersecurity roles, including cybersecurity engineer.
  • Leadership and mentorship skills.
  • Advanced certifications in cybersecurity (e.g., CEH).
  • Expertise in designing and implementing robust security solutions.
  • Knowledge of compliance and regulatory standards.
 

6. Long-Term Goal: Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)

Job Requirements and Qualifications

  • Extensive cybersecurity experience, including senior roles.
  • Advanced degrees in cybersecurity or related fields (e.g., Master's or CISSP).
  • Strategic thinking and risk management skills.
  • Excellent communication and leadership abilities.
  • Proven track record in leading security initiatives.
