READING

MY SUMMARY

Twelve Pillars

By Jim Rohn and Chris Widener

Twelve Pillars by Jim Rohn and Chris Widener is a story of personal development and success. It follows Michael, a salesperson, who learns from Charlie, a gardener, the twelve pillars that can transform one's life.

These pillars encompass:

  • Personal growth
  • Well-being
  • Relationships
  • Goal achievement
  • Time management
  • Networking
  • Continuous learning
  • Financial development
  • Effective communication
  • Giving back
  • Leaving a legacy
  • Embracing adventure

WHAT I LEARNT FROM 12 PILLARS?

1
Personal Development

As a computer networking and security student, continuous learning and skill development are crucial. I Embrace this pillar by staying updated with the latest trends in cybersecurity, attending relevant seminars, and seeking opportunities for personal growth in my field.

2
Total Well-Being

Balancing my academic pursuits with a healthy lifestyle is essential. I prioritize physical fitness, mental well-being, and maintain a positive attitude to excel in my studies and future career.

3
The Gift of Relationships

Building strong connections in the tech industry is vital.I am proactive in networking,and always try to maintain a respectful and honest demeanor. I also seek mentors who can guide me on my career path.

4
Achieve Goals

I have defined clear career goals and created a roadmap to achieve them. I regularly assess my progress and celebrate milestones along the way.

5
Proper Use of Time

Time management is crucial in my field. I learnt to prioritize tasks, minimize distractions, and focus on my studies and skill development.

6
Only the Best People

I choose mentors and peers who inspire me and can help me grow in my field. I collaborate with like-minded individuals who share my passion for cybersecurity.

7
Be a Lifelong Learner

I stay up-to-date with cybersecurity advancements, and I'm considering to pursue the relevant certifications as I progress in my studies and career.

8
Income Seldom Exceeds Personal Development

I understand that my earning potential is directly linked to my expertise and value in the job market.I will continuously invest in my skills to increase my worth.

9
Communication Brings Common Ground

Effective communication is vital in cybersecurity.I will continue to hone my communication skills, both technical and interpersonal, to excel in my future career.

10
The Power of Giving

As I gain knowledge and experience, I'll give back to the cybersecurity community.I'll mentor and share my insights with others without expecting anything in return.

11
Leave a Legacy

I'll strive to leave a positive impact through my work in cybersecurity. I'll uphold ethical standards and contribute to a safer digital world.

12
Live a Life of Adventure

I'll embrace new challenges and opportunities in my career.I don't fear taking calculated risks to achieve my goals.

CONCLUSION

In pursuit of a Bachelor of Science in Computer Networking and Security with a major in Cybersecurity, my journey has been marked by continuous growth and skill development. Along this path, I have honed invaluable skills that include expertise in network infrastructure, proficiency in cybersecurity principles, and effective communication abilities. These skills, coupled with my academic endeavors, have positioned me to excel in the dynamic realm of computer networking and security, as I continue to embrace new challenges and opportunities on the road to success.
